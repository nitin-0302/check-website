About the Fest

For the Students, By the Students

Rasayan is an annual inter-collegiate fest that serves as a platform for students to apply their knowledge of Chemistry in an engaging and enjoyable manner. This year's theme, Panchtatva, celebrates the five natural elements—Earth, Water, Fire, Air, and Space—and their vital role in sustainability and Green Chemistry.

Participate, compete, create, and grow. Rasayan 25–26 awaits!
